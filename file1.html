WHAT IS FLOWER AND WHY ITS IMPORTANT IN OUR LIFE

Flower is a plant organ. It is reproductive structure of an angiospermous plant. It is a determinate, modefied shoot having either or both a stamen and pistil where male and female sexual garmentes are produced, respectively. It is the pistil which develops into a seed or seeds enclosed in fruits. In addition, this plant organ may also consist of outer whorls of petals and/or sepals.
